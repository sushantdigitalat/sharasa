Introducing the 5-Day Assessment Program

Our 5-day program is carefully structured to provide a comprehensive assessment of your child's development across six key areas. Each day focuses on one specific area, with engaging activities and expert advice to help you support your child’s growth.

Physical Development

Evaluate your child’s motor skills, physical co-ordination, and overall health.

Psychological Development

Understand your child’s emotional well-being and mental resilience.

Intellectual Development

Discover your child’s cognitive abilities and problem-solving skills.

Social Development

Learn about your child’s social interactions, friendships, and communication.

Moral & Ethical Development

Assess your child’s values, principles, and understanding of right and wrong.
